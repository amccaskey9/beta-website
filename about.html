B A C K G R O U N D

I started this journey as a lost, twenty something who wanted an “unorthodox” life for myself - where I didn't have to log 40 hours a week and schedule my life around work. I wanted to be doing something I truly enjoyed, without the worry of financial stability, and have a good quality of life.
The first time I seen code was on Tumblr in themes around 2013. I revisited that interest at the end of 2021 and started researching the best free ways to learn code. I found Codecademy and used their free version for a few months to get my feet wet. I enjoyed it, but I needed something with guided hands-on experience. February 2022, my sister sent me a link to SheCodes. At the time, I had just quit my job and decided to take a moment to really think about what I wanted in life. I was hesitant to pay for a workshop to learn coding for a few reasons:
  • I was unemployed and that money could be used for something else
  • I could finish this workshop more confused than before
A week after I visited the website, I decided I had nothing to lose by investing in myself and took the dive. I completed the workshop on time, despite life happening and starting a few days late, and everything changed. From there, I kept finding ways to learn code and practice what I learned. I set a goal to be confident in calling myself a front end developer by my 26th birthday and went for it.
Big thanks to Matt and the team at SheCodes for helping me find my passion and turn my dreams into a reality!
